Home

This is the home of the London Python Project Nights group and the place to find general information about us. Information about specific events can be found in the Events section of the site. This is very much an open-source meet-up - if you have any input on either our site or our meet-up feel free to get improved, whether through attendance of our events, through the mailing list or on twitter, or through GitHub pull requests. All constructive input is welcomed, even if not accepted.

Staying informed:

To chat about the meetup please email london-python-project-nights@googlegroups.com or to sign up to the mailing list and get notified of what's going on:

If you have a google account:

Go to the group page and hit the Join button. This will allow you to view topics via the web interface and set your email notification preferences.

Alternatively:

Send an email to the group owners with the subject "Sign me up", and we will add your email to the group's member list - you can't view the email archive this way, but you will get group emails going forward, and can respond just like with any other mailing list.
