Staying informed:

To chat about the meetup please email london-python-project-nights@googlegroups.com . To sign up to the mailing list and get notified of what's going on:

If you have a google account:

Go to the group page and hit the Join button. This will allow you to view topics via the web interface and set your email notification preferences.

Alternatively:

Send an email to the group owners with the subject "Sign me up", and we will add your email to the group's member list - you can't view the email archive this way, but you will get group emails going forward, and can respond just like with any other mailing list.
